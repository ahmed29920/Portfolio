About

I'm a backend engineer focused on building scalable, secure Laravel applications. I care about writing clean, maintainable code, and I pay close attention to the architectural decisions that keep systems stable as they grow.
Right now I'm a Backend Team Lead at QeemaTech, where I oversee architecture, lead code reviews, and mentor developers on the team.
A big part of my role is setting the technical direction for our projects and making sure good practices are baked into how we build, not just bolted on afterward.
I've worked on a range of projects before this — multi-vendor e-commerce platforms, ride-hailing apps, real-time systems — and built things like reusable authentication packages, bilingual search systems, and ranking engines for sponsored ads. I've also spent time as an instructor teaching Laravel and React, which shaped how I think about building systems that hold up technically while still being practical to scale.
I'm a quick learner and I'm always looking to improve my skills and knowledge. I'm a team player and I'm always looking to help my team succeed. I'm a problem solver and I'm always looking to find the best solution for the problem. I'm a communicator and I'm always looking to communicate with my team and clients to understand their needs and goals.
